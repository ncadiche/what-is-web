What is a website...

I pondered this question for many days.

Could a website be a plant with its many vines like connecting links and its leaves like files and destinations.
Could a website be a cloud made up of hundreds of files and assets like the water droplets painting beautiful scenes across the sky.
Could a website be a train track linking people, ideas, and information from across the world to one another through one central system with hundreds of smaller connections.

What is a website... and that's when it came to me.

A website has no definition. It has no concrete meaning. It has no set resctrictions or restraints for what it should or should not be. A website is art. It is information. It is an introduction to its creator. It is a platform for communication.

A website is what a website wants to be. And thats why I made this website for thee...
